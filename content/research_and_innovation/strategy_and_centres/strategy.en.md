## Research Strategy

### Vision

To position Al-Qadisiyah University as a distinguished research center regionally and internationally.

### Objectives

1. Increase internationally published research output
2. Strengthen research partnerships
3. Support researchers and fund projects
4. Link research to community service

### Research Priorities

- Sustainable energy
- Public health
- Information technology
- Agriculture and food security
- Environment and water resources
