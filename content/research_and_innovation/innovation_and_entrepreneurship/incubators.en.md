## Al-Qadisiyah University Business Incubator

The Al-Qadisiyah University Business Incubator helps transform research and creative ideas into successful commercial ventures, and connects graduates with opportunities in industry and services.

## Services

### For students and graduates
- Entrepreneurship workshops and training
- Mentoring and support in developing business models
- Co-working spaces
- Seed funding for selected projects

### For startups
- 6–12 months of hosting and funding
- Mentoring from experts and specialised coaches
- Access to university laboratories and equipment
- A network of industrial partners

## Tracks

### Technology & AI
In partnership with the College of Computer Science and Information Technology.

### Food & Agricultural Industries
In partnership with the College of Agriculture and the College of Biotechnology.

### Medical & Health Technologies
In partnership with the colleges of Medicine, Pharmacy, and Dentistry.

### Creative Industries
In partnership with the College of Fine Arts and the College of Arts.

## Partnerships

- Ministry of Higher Education — "Ibtikar" innovation programme
- Iraqi Federation of Industries
- International partners through exchange programmes
- Iraqi banks and funding institutions
