## Accessibility Statement

Al-Qadisiyah University is committed to making its website accessible to all users.

### Our Commitments

- Design compliant with WCAG 2.1 standards
- Screen reader support
- Appropriate color contrast
- Alternative text for images
- Keyboard navigation

We welcome your feedback to improve accessibility.
