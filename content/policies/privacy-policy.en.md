## Introduction

Al-Qadisiyah University is committed to protecting the privacy of its website visitors and users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Name and contact details when filling out contact forms
- Email address when subscribing to newsletters
- Academic information when applying for admission

### Automatically Collected Information
- IP address and browser type
- Pages visited on the website
- Date and time of visit
- Cookies

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery**: Processing admission and registration applications
2. **Communication**: Responding to inquiries and sending important notifications
3. **Website Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
4. **Security**: Protecting the website from unauthorized use

---

## Data Protection

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

- Use of encrypted HTTPS protocol
- Storage of data on secure servers
- Restricted access to authorized personnel only
- Regular updates to security systems

---

## Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

- Remember language preferences
- Improve website performance
- Analyze traffic

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of data
- Object to data processing

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except in the following cases:

- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal requirements
- To protect the university's rights
