## Data Protection Policy

Al-Qadisiyah University is committed to protecting personal data of all users according to international standards.

### Data Protection Principles

1. **Transparency** - Informing users how their data is used
2. **Minimization** - Collecting only necessary data
3. **Accuracy** - Maintaining data accuracy and updates
4. **Security** - Protecting data from unauthorized access

### Data Subject Rights

- Right to access data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
