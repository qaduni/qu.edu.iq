## Al-Qadisiyah University Apps

Al-Qadisiyah University's official apps are available on the iOS App Store and Google Play. Students, faculty, and staff can access digital university services directly from their phones.

## Available Apps

### Al-Qadisiyah University Official App
The university's primary mobile portal — includes:

- Latest news and announcements
- Quick access to the Student Portal (SIS)
- Academic calendar
- College and department information
- Campus map
- Contact directory

## System Requirements

- **iOS:** Version 13 or later
- **Android:** Version 8.0 or later
- Internet connection required for most features
