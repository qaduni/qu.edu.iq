## Commitment to the SDGs

Al-Qadisiyah University is committed to advancing the [17 UN Sustainable Development Goals](https://sdgs.un.org/) adopted by the United Nations in 2015. The university operates a dedicated portal documenting these contributions and the underlying initiatives in detail.

> **Sustainability portal:** [sust.qu.edu.iq](https://sust.qu.edu.iq)

## Areas of Contribution

### SDG 4 — Quality Education
- Inclusive, equitable higher education for all segments of society
- Scholarships for low-income and international students
- Lifelong learning through the [Continuing Education Centre](/en/campus/continuing-education/)

### SDG 5 — Gender Equality
- Women constitute more than 50% of the student body
- A dedicated College of Education for Women
- Programmes supporting women in academic leadership

### SDG 3 — Good Health and Well-being
- Colleges of Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy, Nursing, and Veterinary Medicine
- Field research in public health, in partnership with the Ministry of Health

### SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
- [Business Incubators](/en/research/incubators/) supporting startups
- Annually registered [Patents](/en/research/patents/)
- Partnerships with Iraq's industrial sector

### SDG 13 — Climate Action
- Campus sustainability initiatives
- Research in renewable energy and energy efficiency

### Other Goals
For contributions toward the remaining goals (1 No Poverty, 2 Zero Hunger, 6 Clean Water, 7 Affordable Energy, 8 Decent Work, 10 Reduced Inequalities, 11 Sustainable Cities, 12 Responsible Consumption, 14 Life Below Water, 15 Life on Land, 16 Peace & Justice, 17 Partnerships), please refer to the main sustainability portal.
