## Vision

To be a regionally pioneering and internationally recognized university in higher education, scientific research, and community service.

## Mission

Al-Qadisiyah University is committed to providing distinguished academic education and preparing qualified and specialized cadres through:

- Providing a stimulating educational environment for creativity and innovation
- Enhancing scientific research aimed at solving community problems
- Instilling ethical and national values in students
- Building effective partnerships with local and international institutions

## Strategic Goals

### 1. Academic Excellence
- Developing academic programs according to international standards
- Recruiting distinguished scientific competencies
- Providing modern and advanced learning resources

### 2. Scientific Research
- Supporting original and applied scientific research
- Encouraging publication in prestigious scientific journals
- Establishing specialized research centers

### 3. Community Service
- Providing consultations and services to public and private sectors
- Organizing continuing education and training programs
- Contributing to solving societal problems

### 4. Digital Transformation
- Digitizing administrative and academic processes
- Developing e-learning platforms
- Enhancing technological infrastructure

### 5. International Cooperation
- Expanding partnerships with global universities
- Exchanging academic and research experiences
- Participating in international conferences and events

## Core Values

- **Integrity**: Commitment to the highest standards of ethics and transparency
- **Excellence**: Continuous pursuit of quality and mastery
- **Innovation**: Encouraging creativity and critical thinking
- **Collaboration**: Working in team spirit and partnership
- **Responsibility**: Commitment to community and nation
