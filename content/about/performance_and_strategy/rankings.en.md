## International Rankings

### Webometrics Ranking
Al-Qadisiyah University holds advanced positions in the Webometrics World Universities Ranking, which measures the digital presence and impact of universities.

### Times Higher Education Ranking
The university is working to achieve an advanced position in THE ranking, which evaluates universities based on teaching, research, citations, and internationalization.

### QS World Rankings
The university is working to improve its indicators to enter the QS World University Rankings.
