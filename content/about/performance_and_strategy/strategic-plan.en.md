## Strategic Plan 2024-2029

### Strategic Vision

To position Al-Qadisiyah University as a leading institution regionally and internationally in education, scientific research, and community service.

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Enhance Education Quality** - Improve academic programs according to international standards
2. **Strengthen Research** - Increase research output published in global databases
3. **Community Service** - Provide programs and services that meet local community needs
4. **Digital Transformation** - Develop technical infrastructure and e-services
5. **Institutional Excellence** - Improve administrative performance and governance
