## Admission Requirements

### General Requirements

1. Iraqi Preparatory Certificate or equivalent
2. Meeting required average for college and major
3. Passing medical examination (for health colleges)
4. No disciplinary dismissal from another university

### Required Documents

- Original graduation certificate with certified copy
- Civil Status ID
- National Card
- Iraqi Nationality Certificate
- Recent passport photos (6 photos)
