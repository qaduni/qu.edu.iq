## International Students - Study in Iraq

Al-Qadisiyah University welcomes international students from around the world.

### Study in Iraq Program

A government program to attract international students to study at Iraqi universities.

### Benefits

- Competitive tuition fees
- University accommodation
- Student visa
- Academic support

### Required Documents

- Valid passport
- Certified academic certificates
- Health certificate
- Passport photos

### Application Steps

1. Register via [Study in Iraq Portal](https://studyiniraq.gov.iq)
2. Choose university and program
3. Submit documents
4. Receive conditional acceptance
5. Complete visa procedures
