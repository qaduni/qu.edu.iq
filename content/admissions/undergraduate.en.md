## Undergraduate Admissions (Bachelor's)

### Available Colleges

The university includes 18 colleges offering bachelor's programs in various specializations.

### Admission Requirements

- Iraqi Preparatory Certificate or equivalent
- Meeting the required average for the college
- Passing medical examination (for health colleges)

### Application Steps

1. Register with Centralized Admission Department
2. Select preferences by priority
3. Follow admission results
4. Complete registration procedures
