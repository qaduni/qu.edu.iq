## Postgraduate Admissions

### Available Programs

- Master's Programs
- Doctoral Programs
- Higher Diploma Programs

### Admission Requirements

#### Master's
- Bachelor's degree with minimum 65% average
- Passing admission test and interview
- English language certificate

#### Doctorate
- Master's degree with minimum 70% average
- Preliminary research proposal
- Academic supervisor approval
