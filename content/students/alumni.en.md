## Alumni Affairs

Al-Qadisiyah University maintains communication with its graduates and supports them in their professional journey.

## Alumni Services

### Alumni Database
- Graduate data registration
- Contact information updates
- Career path tracking

### Employment Opportunities
- Job announcements from partner companies
- Annual job fairs
- Academic recommendations and references

### Professional Development
- Workshops and training courses
- Career counseling programs
- Continuing education opportunities

### Certificates and Documents
- Certificate copies
- Document authentication
- Official transcripts

## Alumni Network

Join the Al-Qadisiyah University alumni network to connect with peers and exchange experiences and opportunities.

### Events
- Annual alumni gatherings
- Conferences and seminars
- Social and sports activities

### Groups
- Groups by college/discipline
- Groups by graduation year
- Professional sector groups

## Network Registration

To register in the alumni database and join the network:

1. Complete the online registration form
2. Attach graduation certificate copy
3. Activate account via email
