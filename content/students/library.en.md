## Electronic Library

The Central Library of Al-Qadisiyah University provides an integrated electronic library system that allows students and researchers access to diverse knowledge resources.

## Library Services

### E-Books
- Thousands of titles in various disciplines
- Arabic and English books
- Reading and download capabilities

### Scientific Databases
- **Scopus**: Peer-reviewed research database
- **IEEE Xplore**: Engineering and technical research
- **PubMed**: Medical and health research
- **JSTOR**: Humanities and social sciences

### Journals and Periodicals
- Peer-reviewed scientific journals
- Daily newspapers and periodicals
- Periodical archives

### Other Services
- Electronic catalog search
- Online book reservation
- Electronic borrowing
- Printing and copying service

## Library Access

### For Students
Use your Student Portal credentials to access the electronic library.

### For Faculty and Researchers
Use your university email to get full access privileges.

[Access E-Library](https://library.qu.edu.iq)
