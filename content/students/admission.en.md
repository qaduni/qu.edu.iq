## Overview

Al-Qadisiyah University welcomes all students wishing to join our academic journey. The university follows centralized admission standards approved by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research.

---

## General Admission Requirements

### For Morning Studies
1. Obtain Iraqi Preparatory School Certificate or equivalent
2. Achieve the required average according to college and major
3. Pass medical examination (for health colleges)
4. No disciplinary dismissal from another university

### For Evening Studies
1. Same requirements as morning studies
2. Required averages differ from morning studies

## Required Documents

- Original graduation certificate with certified copy
- Civil Status ID
- National Card
- Iraqi Nationality Certificate
- Recent passport photos (6 photos)
- Centralized admission application form

## Application Stages

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Online Registration] --> B[Select Preferences]
    B --> C[Results Announcement]
    C --> D[Confirm Admission]
    D --> E[Submit Documents]
```

1. **Online Registration**: Through Centralized Admission Department website
2. **Select Preferences**: Rank colleges by priority
3. **Results Announcement**: Publication of admission results
4. **Confirm Admission**: Confirm acceptance
5. **Submit Documents**: Complete registration file
