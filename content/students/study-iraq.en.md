## Study in Iraq Program

Al-Qadisiyah University welcomes international students from around the world to study in its diverse academic programs.

## Why Al-Qadisiyah University?

- **Quality Education**: Accredited and advanced academic programs
- **Affordable Tuition**: Compared to regional universities
- **Rich Cultural Environment**: Opportunity to learn about Iraq's ancient civilization
- **Strategic Location**: In the heart of Iraq with easy access

## Programs for International Students

### Bachelor's Programs
- Human Medicine (6 years)
- Dentistry (5 years)
- Pharmacy (5 years)
- Engineering (4 years)
- Sciences (4 years)

### Graduate Programs
- Master's in various disciplines
- PhD in various disciplines

## Admission Requirements

1. High school certificate or equivalent
2. Certificate equivalency from relevant authorities
3. Proof of language proficiency (Arabic or English)
4. Meeting program-specific requirements

## Application Steps

1. **Online Registration**: Through Study in Iraq website
2. **Upload Documents**: Required certificates and documents
3. **Pay Application Fee**: Through electronic portal
4. **Await Admission**: Application review and results announcement
5. **Visa Issuance**: After final admission
